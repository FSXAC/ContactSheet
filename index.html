Contact Sheet Generator

This tool generates a contact sheet from a folder of images with realistic film borders.

It is currently in development, version 0.0. Made by Muchen.

To use, upload a folder of pictures (recommended between 12-40). You'll get a image preview once all images are done loading. Click the "Preview" button to preview the filmstrip.
